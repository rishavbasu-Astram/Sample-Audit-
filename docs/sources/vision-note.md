**Financial System for ASTRAM**

**① Financial Accounting — (External) Reporting**

This side of the system is needed to ensure the company complies with legal &
regulatory standards (like the IFRS or US GAAP) & to track money in & out.

- **General Ledger:** The central brain of the accounting system. Every transaction
  across the company automatically flows here to create the chart of accounts, balance
  sheets & profit & loss (P&L) statements.

Now there are a total of **9 features** to implement — so that we can call this the
perfect alternative of **SAP Finance**:

1. Real-Time Financial Dashboard & Unified Ledger
2. General Ledger Management
3. Accounts Payable
4. Accounts Receivable
5. Asset Management & Depreciation
6. Cost Center Accounting
7. Product Cost Controlling
8. Profitability Analysis
9. Cash & Bank Reconciliation
